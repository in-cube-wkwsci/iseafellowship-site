About

What is the i-SEA Fellowship?

A hybrid month-long programme designed to equip content creators with the knowledge, skills, and tools to become ethical and impactful digital storytellers.

The Influence for Impact in Southeast Asia (i-SEA) Fellowship Programme is an initiative of the Centre for Information Integrity and the Internet (IN-cube) at the Wee Kim Wee School of Communication and Information, Nanyang Technological University (NTU).

We invite applications from aspiring student creators who are ready to engage in important conversations on information integrity, digital media ethics, and the power of storytelling in the age of AI.

Through online masterclasses, mentorship, and a week-long in-person workshop and hackathon in Singapore, fellows will explore key topics including:

Online harms & misinformation Media law & ethics AI in storytelling Fact-checking tools Immersive journalism Hackathon challenges

Programme

One month. Three phases.

💻

Online Masterclasses

Three weeks of sessions on misinformation, media ethics, AI literacy, and fact-checking with experts from journalism, tech, and academia.

🏢

Industry Visits

Go behind the scenes at TikTok's Transparency Centre, SPH Media newsroom, and MediaCorp broadcast studios in Singapore.

🚀

Hackathon

Work in cross-cultural teams to develop solutions for real information integrity challenges, mentored by industry experts.

Eligibility

Who can apply

Basic requirements

  • Age: 18 to 25 years old
  • Nationality: Southeast Asian citizen
  • Education: Currently enrolled in higher education
  • Endorsement: Nominated by your institution

Creator requirements

  • Following: 1,000+ followers on any platform
  • Activity: Regular monthly posting history
  • Platform: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, or other
  • Commitment: Available for full programme
